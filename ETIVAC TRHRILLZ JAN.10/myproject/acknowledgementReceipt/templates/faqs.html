Frequently Asked Questions

What are the park hours?

The park is open from 9 AM to 10 PM daily.

What is the ticket price?

General admission is $50 for adults and $30 for children under 12.

Are there any height restrictions for rides?

Yes, guests must be at least 48 inches tall to ride most attractions.

Can I bring outside food and drinks?

No, outside food and beverages are not allowed in the park.

Is parking available?

Yes, there is ample parking available for a fee of $10 per vehicle.
